How To Use

  1. Start Camera and allow permission when asked
  2. Print an Original ArUco marker (20–25 cm works best up to 10 ft)
  3. Enter your marker size and stand at your calibration distance, then click Set Calibration
  4. Press Start Recording, then move toward or away from the camera
  5. Press Stop, then Export CSV to save your data
